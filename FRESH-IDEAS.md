### AI Query Examples Library
**Date Added**: 2025-11-17  
**Priority**: High  
**Category**: Content  
**Status**: üí° New Idea  
**Estimated Effort**: Large

- Could be its own dedicated page or integrated into guides

**Related PRs/Sections**:
