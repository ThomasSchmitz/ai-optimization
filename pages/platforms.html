Organizations that win in the AI era will be those that intelligently route tasks, queries, and content to the appropriate AI platform. This requires dedicated ownership ‚Äî someone in every organization now needs to manage the routing layer.[1]

AI Operations: The New Organizational Function

As organizations scale their AI usage, AI Operations becomes a real headcount function requiring dedicated platform teams to manage:[1]

  • Routing strategy: Which tasks go to which AI platforms
  • Prompt maintenance: Optimizing and updating prompts across platforms
  • Tool governance: Managing access, costs, and compliance
  • Team education: Training staff on platform-specific best practices
  • Performance monitoring: Tracking citation rates and visibility
  • Safety and compliance: Ensuring responsible AI usage
