About E-E-A-T

E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness) is a framework originally from Google's Search Quality Guidelines, but it's increasingly important for all AI platforms when evaluating content quality and citation-worthiness.

üíº

Experience

First-hand, practical experience with the topic. Real-world knowledge demonstrated through specific examples and insights.

üéì

Expertise

Deep knowledge and qualifications in the subject matter. Demonstrated through credentials, detailed analysis, and technical accuracy.

üèÖ

Authoritativeness

Recognition as a go-to source in your field. Built through backlinks, mentions, awards, and industry recognition.

üîí

Trustworthiness

Accuracy, transparency, and reliability. Demonstrated through citations, fact-checking, clear policies, and security measures.

Why E-E-A-T Matters for AI Platforms

AI platforms prioritize high-quality, trustworthy content when generating responses. Strong E-E-A-T signals increase your likelihood of being cited and recommended.

Citation Quality

AI platforms prefer citing authoritative, expert sources. High E-E-A-T makes your content more citation-worthy.

Training Data Selection

When AI models are trained or updated, they prioritize high-quality sources with strong trust signals.

Recommendation Confidence

AI assistants are more confident recommending content from established, trustworthy sources.

Competitive Advantage

As AI becomes more sophisticated, E-E-A-T signals will increasingly determine content visibility.
