AI-Ready Content Best Practices

Follow these guidelines to create content that AI platforms are more likely to cite and recommend:

Clear Structure

Use descriptive headings (H2, H3), short paragraphs, bullet points, and clear topic organization.

Conversational Tone

Write naturally, address questions directly, and use language your audience would use when asking questions.

Demonstrate Expertise

Include specific examples, data, case studies, and unique insights that show deep knowledge.

Cite Sources

Link to authoritative external sources. AI platforms value well-researched content with citations.

Answer Questions

Structure content around common questions. Include FAQ sections and direct question-answer formats.

Be Comprehensive

Cover topics thoroughly. Longer, in-depth content (1500+ words) performs better in AI citations.
