Common Schema Types for AI Optimization

Different schema types serve different purposes. Here are the most important for AI visibility:

Organization

Essential for establishing your business identity. Include name, logo, contact info, and social profiles.

Product

Critical for e-commerce. Include detailed descriptions, prices, reviews, and availability.

Article / BlogPosting

For content pages. Include author, publish date, and comprehensive article information.

FAQPage

Optimizes for voice search and AI queries. Structure questions and answers clearly.

LocalBusiness

For local visibility. Include address, hours, service areas, and contact details.

HowTo

Step-by-step content optimization. AI platforms love structured instructions.
