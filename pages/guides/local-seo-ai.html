Introduction

Local SEO for AI platforms requires a different approach than traditional local SEO. AI assistants like Google Assistant, Siri, Alexa, and Gemini prioritize specific signals when determining which local businesses to recommend. This guide covers advanced strategies specifically designed for AI-powered local search.

How AI Platforms Evaluate Local Businesses

  • Proximity: Distance from the user's location
  • Relevance: How well your business matches the query
  • Prominence: How well-known and trustworthy your business is
  • Structured Data: Quality and completeness of schema markup
  • Reviews: Quantity, quality, and recency of reviews
  • Engagement: User interaction with your listings
  • Freshness: How recently your information has been updated

Local AI Ranking Factors

Understanding what AI platforms prioritize in local search is essential for optimization success. The primary signals include proximity (35%), relevance (30%), prominence (20%), and data quality (15%).
