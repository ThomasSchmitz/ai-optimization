Optimization Strategies for Arc

The best way to optimize for Arc is to create pages that AI can summarize easily, while still rewarding users who click through.

1. Design for Summaries

  • Use clear H2/H3s that describe each section‚Äôs main takeaway.
  • Include bullet lists for key steps, pros/cons, and checklists.
  • Add a TL;DR or summary section Arc can lift directly.

2. Make Mobile the Default

  • Test pages on small screens; avoid horizontal scrolling.
  • Use readable font sizes and adequate line spacing.
  • Keep interactive elements large enough for touch.

3. Strengthen Topical Authority

  • Publish clusters of related content around key topics.
  • Interlink guides, glossaries, and case studies.
  • Demonstrate E-E-A-T with author bios and source citations.

4. Optimize for Speed & Stability

  • Compress images and defer non-critical scripts.
  • Avoid heavy popups or modals that could block content.
  • Monitor Core Web Vitals, especially on mobile connections.
