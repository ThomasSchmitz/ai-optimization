## The Global AI Landscape

 While American AI platforms like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude dominate global headlines, 
 regional and national AI platforms are thriving in their respective markets. These platforms 
 are often better suited to local languages, regulations, cultural contexts, and user preferences.
