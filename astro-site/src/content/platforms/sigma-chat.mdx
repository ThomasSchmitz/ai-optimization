## What is Sigma Chat?

 Sigma Chat is an AI search engine and browsing assistant that behaves more like an 
 iterative research partner than a one-shot answer box. It keeps track of your previous 
 questions, suggests follow-ups, and can spin its findings into content drafts.

### Research-Oriented UX

 Sigma Chat is built around long-running conversations. Users start broad, then 
 narrow over time. Your brand wins visibility when your content supports the entire 
 research journey ‚Äî not just a single query.

 üß© 
 
### Context Memory

 The engine remembers prior prompts and incorporates that context into subsequent 
 answers. That means consistent terminology, clear internal linking, and reusable 
 definitions on your site pay off more with each follow-up question.

### Content Creation Hooks

 Sigma Chat can turn research outputs into outlines, blog posts, and social content. 
 Pages that read like strong source material for content briefs are more likely to be 
 surfaced and referenced.

## Sigma Chat Optimization Checklist

 Use this checklist to confirm that your content ecosystem is ready for deep, iterative 
 AI search.

### ‚úÖ Topic Clusters

 - Pillar pages published for your 3‚Äì5 core topics.

 - Supporting articles linked to their parent pillars.

 - Each key topic includes beginner, intermediate, and expert content.

### ‚úÖ Research Depth

 - Pages include benchmarks, ranges, and specific examples.

 - Claims are supported by citations to authoritative sources.

 - Pros and cons are clearly documented for major options.

### ‚úÖ Navigation & Internal Links

 - Every article links to at least 2‚Äì3 deeper resources.

 - Glossary or explainer pages exist for key concepts.

 - Breadcrumbs or nav elements reflect your topic structure.

### ‚úÖ Schema & Structure

 - Article schema implemented on major guides.

 - FAQPage schema used for high-value Q&A sections.

 - Breadcrumb schema present on pillar and cluster pages.

### ‚úÖ Content for Creators

 - Articles include key takeaways and angle ideas.

 - Frameworks and step-by-step processes are clearly described.

 - Examples and case snippets can be repurposed into outlines.
