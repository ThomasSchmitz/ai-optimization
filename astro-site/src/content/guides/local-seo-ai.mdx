## Introduction

 Local SEO for AI platforms requires a different approach than traditional local SEO. AI assistants 
 like Google Assistant, Siri, Alexa, and Gemini prioritize specific signals when determining which 
 local businesses to recommend. This guide covers advanced strategies specifically designed for 
 AI-powered local search.

### How AI Platforms Evaluate Local Businesses

- **Proximity:** Distance from the user's location
- **Relevance:** How well your business matches the query
- **Prominence:** How well-known and trustworthy your business is
- **Structured Data:** Quality and completeness of schema markup
- **Reviews:** Quantity, quality, and recency of reviews
- **Engagement:** User interaction with your listings
- **Freshness:** How recently your information has been updated

### What You'll Learn

- The critical ranking factors for local AI recommendations
- How to optimize for proximity-based queries
- Creating locally-focused content that AI understands
- Building and managing citations across the web

- Measuring and tracking local AI visibility

## Local AI Ranking Factors

 Understanding what AI platforms prioritize in local search is essential for optimization success. 
 The primary signals include proximity (35%), relevance (30%), prominence (20%), and data quality (15%).
